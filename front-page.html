ABOUT US

For 10 years, Just Dance has been introducingthe art of dance to people of all ages!

Just Dance Studio was launched in 2010. Over the years, we have become a close-knit artistic troupe, and many of the women and men who had attended our classes became participants in world dance shows. Just Dance Studio has the best teachers and a huge number of types of dances. We will help you immerse yourself in the music, and not only hear but also feel it!
